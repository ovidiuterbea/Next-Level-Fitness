Client routes: 

	/api/clients
		
		GET .../ (Retrieve a list of clients) BUNA CONECTATA
		GET .../:clientid (Retrieve data for a client by his id) BUNA CONECTATA
		POST .../signup (Create a new client) BUNA CONECTATA
		POST .../login (Log client in) BUNA CONECTATA
		PATCH .../subscription/:clientid (Create a subscription for a client or create it) BUNA CONECTATA
		PATCH .../subscriptionDel/:clientid (Delete the subscription by the user id) BUNA
		PATCH .../:clientid/trainer/:trainerid (Give a personal trainer to a client by the client id) (push to client and trainer) BUNA 
		PATCH .../:clientid/trainerDel/:trainerid(Delete a personal trainer of a client by the client id) (pull from client and trainer)  BUNA 
		PATCH .../:clientid/class/:classid (Give a client a class)  (push to client and class) BUNA CONECTATA
		PATCH .../:clientid/classDel/:classid(Delete class from client) (pull from client and class) BUNA
